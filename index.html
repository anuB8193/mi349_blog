Biryani: A Timeless Indian Dish

October 10, 2024 by Anushka Basani

Biryani is more than just a dish; it’s an experience of flavors, textures, and aromas that delight the senses. Originating in India, this timeless rice dish has won hearts all over the world. The key to a perfect biryani is using the right blend of spices and the best-quality basmati rice. Here’s a simple chicken biryani recipe you can try at home.

To start, marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger, garlic, turmeric, cumin, and garam masala for at least two hours. Then, cook onions until golden, and layer the marinated chicken over the onions in a large pot. Parboil the rice and spread it over the chicken, adding saffron-infused milk and fresh herbs like cilantro and mint. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 30-40 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked, and the rice is tender.

Biryani is typically served with a cooling raita made from yogurt and cucumbers, along with a tangy pickle on the side. Enjoy this rich and flavorful dish for special occasions or when you crave something comforting!

Smiski: The Adorable Glow-in-the-Dark Collectibles

October 5, 2024 by Anushka Basani

Have you ever heard of Smiski? These tiny glow-in-the-dark figures have captured the attention of collectors and fans around the world. Smiski figures come in various poses, and their primary quirk is that they love to hide in small spaces around your home. Whether sitting on a shelf, hiding in a corner, or perched on a book, these little creatures add a touch of whimsy to your everyday life.

What makes Smiski figures even more special is their glow-in-the-dark feature. By day, they blend into the background with their soft, pastel-like appearance, but once the lights are off, they radiate a gentle glow, creating a magical atmosphere. These figures come in blind boxes, meaning you won’t know which pose you’ve gotten until you open the box—adding an element of surprise to each purchase.

Smiski has gained popularity not just as cute desk decorations but also as stress relievers. Their simple design and calming glow can offer a sense of comfort. Whether you're looking to collect them all or just want a little friend to keep you company, Smiski figures are a delightful addition to any space.
